Head of the Department

Dr. Harini P

Dr. Harini P is a distinguished academician and researcher with over 27 years of experience in the field of Computer Science and Engineering. She has published numerous research papers in reputed international journals and has been instrumental in guiding students towards excellence in both academics and research.

Under her leadership, the CSE department has achieved remarkable milestones, including state-of-the-art labs, industry collaborations, and high placement records.
